# Revenue Analysis

## Objective
Analyze revenue performance over time to identify growth patterns, seasonality, and business momentum.

---

## Methodology
Revenue analysis was performed using:
- Orders dataset
- Order items dataset

KPIs analyzed:
- Monthly Revenue
- Monthly Order Volume
- Average Order Value (AOV)

Formula used:
Revenue = Order Volume × Average Order Value

---

## Key Findings

### 1. Rapid Revenue Growth During 2017
Revenue increased from **111.8K in Jan 2017** to **987.8K in Nov 2017**, showing strong business expansion.

### 2. Significant Seasonal Spike in November 2017
November recorded the highest revenue and order volume, indicating strong seasonal demand, likely due to promotional events.

### 3. Revenue Stabilized in 2018
After rapid growth, revenue stabilized between **826K–977K per month**, suggesting the business entered a mature growth phase.

### 4. Order Volume Was the Main Growth Driver
Order volume increased nearly **9.7x** between Jan and Nov 2017.

### 5. AOV Remained Stable
Average Order Value remained relatively stable between **125–150**, indicating customer spending per transaction did not change significantly.

---

## Business Insights

- Revenue growth was primarily driven by customer acquisition and increased transactions.
- Customer spending behavior remained relatively stable.
- Seasonal sales events had significant impact on business performance.

---

## Recommendations

- Investigate strategies behind November peak performance.
- Improve retention to sustain order growth.
- Analyze customer segments contributing most to revenue.
